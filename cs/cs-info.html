About the Department

The Computer Science department offers graduate programs designed to build strong foundations in software, systems, and applied computing. Students explore core topics such as algorithms, operating systems, databases, networking, and software engineering.

The department supports research and hands-on learning through projects, labs, and industry-focused coursework. Students can tailor their degree toward areas like data engineering, AI, cybersecurity, and large-scale systems.
